    Mini Rack Build

    Info

    This is a photo gallery documenting the process of building my mini-rack in January 2026.
    Parts:

    • GeeekPi: DeskPi Rack-Mate T0 (4U - 10" Rack)
    • 3D-printed brackets for the ZimaBoard 2 NAS (ZimaOS) and Ugreen Switch (Material: ABS)
    • 3D-printed vented blank plates for the front and rear (Material: ABS)

    Components:

    • ZimaBoard 2 1664
    • Ugreen CM753 switch
    • Hitron Modem
    • One SFP+ -> 10GbE transciever - Amazon
    • Two Cat6a Keystone jacks - Amazon
    • Three GeeekPi 6" Cat6a patch cables - Amazon
    • Three Monoprice 3' Cat6a patch cables - Amazon
    • One PCIe 4.0 -> NVMe add-in card - Amazon
    • One SK-Hynix 500GB NVMe SSD (for Docker / VM storage, pulled from ThinkPad after storage upgrade)
    • Two Crucial BX500 4TB SATA SSDs in RAID1 (for mass network attached storage) - Amazon
    • One 30 cm SATA extension cable - Amazon
    • One PWM Fan controller - Amazon
    • Two server-grade 5000 RPM, 80 mm Arctic PWM fans - Amazon
    • Black rack screws - Amazon

    Printing the Parts

    Printed Parts:

    • RackMate bottom horizontal frame - Printables
    • ZimaBoard 2 w/SSD mounting bracket - Printables
    • Ugreen CM753 mounting bracket - (No longer available to download)
    • Vented blank plates (hexagon pattern) - Printables
